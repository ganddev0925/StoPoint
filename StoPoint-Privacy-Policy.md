# Privacy Policy for StoPoint
**Effective Date:** February 27, 2026

---

## 1. Our Story and Promise
StoPoint was not built by a massive corporation; it was created by a solo developer. The app was originally designed as a personal tool to track our child’s therapy classes and calculate the time and costs associated with them.

> **Our Mission:** We know firsthand how expensive therapies and specialized care can be. StoPoint is intentionally designed to be **"Privacy-First"** and **"Zero-Cost."** There are no subscriptions, no ads, and no data-selling. We believe that a tool meant to help you manage your life shouldn’t be another financial burden.

Privacy is our foundational principle. StoPoint **does not** collect, harvest, sell, or upload your personal data to any external servers.

---

## 2. Information We Collect and How We Use It
StoPoint is designed to be functional even without granting sensitive permissions. **All data remains strictly on your device.**

### Location Data (Optional)
* **Auto-Tracking:** If you choose to grant "Always" location access, StoPoint uses Geofencing to automatically log visits.
* **Manual Mode (No Permission Required):** You may use StoPoint entirely without location permissions. You can manually search for places, drop pins, and log visits yourself. No location data is accessed in this mode.

### Camera and Media/Photos
Used for the **OCR (Optical Character Recognition)** feature. 
* Scan receipts, bills, or medical reports to link them to a visit. 
* Processing happens **entirely on your device**. 
* Images and PDFs are stored locally and are never uploaded to external servers.

### Costs and Financial Data
You may manually enter costs to track spending. This data is used only to generate your local monthly expense reports.

### Export Feature
The app allows you to export your data into a **PDF or CSV file**. This process is user-initiated. The resulting file is saved to your device's local storage; we do not have access to these files.

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security
* **100% Local & Encrypted:** We do not have our own cloud servers. Every place, visit, cost, and document is stored in a local database directly on your phone.
* **No Cloud Sync:** Because we do not use cloud storage, nobody (including the developer) can access your data.
* **Document Security:** Attachments linked to visits are kept in the app's private storage folder on your device.

## 5. Advertisements and Tracking
* **Zero Ads:** StoPoint is completely ad-free.
* **Zero Tracking:** We do not monitor your behavior or use third-party analytics.

---

## 6. Data Retention and Deletion
Because your data is only stored locally, you dictate how long it is kept.

* **To delete specific data:** Delete individual visits, attachments, or places directly within the app.
* **To delete all data:** Wipe all data instantly by clearing the app’s data in your Android settings or by uninstalling the app.

---

## 7. Age Restrictions & Children’s Privacy
StoPoint is a general audience utility app intended strictly for use by adults (**aged 18 and over**). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are under 18, please do not use this app.
