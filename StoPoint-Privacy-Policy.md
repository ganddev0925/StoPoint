# Privacy Policy for StoPoint
**Effective Date:** March 9, 2026

---

## 1. Our Story and Promise
StoPoint was created by a solo developer as a personal tool to manage family schedules and therapy logs. 

> **Our Mission:** We believe tools meant to help you manage your life shouldn’t be a financial or privacy burden. StoPoint is intentionally **“Privacy-First”** and **“Zero-Cost.”** We do not have servers, we do not show ads, and we do not sell data.

Privacy is our foundational principle. StoPoint **does not** collect, harvest, or upload your personal data to any external servers.

---

## 2. Information We Collect and How We Use It

### Location Data (Background Access)
* **Purpose:** To automatically log visits, StoPoint requires "Allow all the time" location access. This allows the app to detect geofence transitions **even when the app is closed or not in use.**
* **Processing:** Location triggers are handled by the device's Operating System. StoPoint only records the entry/exit time and the place name to your local database.
* **Platinum Guard (Direct Boot):** To ensure reliability after a phone reboot, a minimal, encrypted cache of geofence events may be stored in "Device Protected" storage until you first unlock your phone.

### Physical Activity Data (NEW)
* **Purpose:** StoPoint requests Physical Activity permission to enable **Smart Silence**. 
* **Use Case:** This allows the app to detect when you are in a vehicle so it can suppress arrival notifications while you are driving, ensuring a distraction-free experience. 
* **Privacy:** This data is processed in real-time on your device and is never stored permanently or shared.

### Camera and Media (Local OCR)
* **Purpose:** Used for the on-device document scanner.
* **Processing:** Images of receipts or PDFs are processed using on-device AI (Google ML Kit). **No images are uploaded to the cloud.** The extracted text and images remain in the app’s private local storage.

### Android Auto
* **Functionality:** StoPoint provides an Android Auto interface to view nearby places and recent visits. 
* **Data Flow:** This data is pulled directly from your phone's local database and displayed on your car's screen. No personal data is shared with the vehicle's manufacturer or third-party car systems.

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security
* **100% Local:** We do not own or operate cloud servers. Your database is stored strictly on your phone.
* **Encryption:** Your local database utilizes Android’s built-in storage encryption.
* **No Cloud Sync:** Because there is no cloud, nobody—including the developer—can access, see, or recover your data. You are the sole owner of your information.
* **Internet Usage (Maps & Search):** StoPoint requires Internet access solely for the Google Maps SDK to display map tiles and perform address searches. **No personal tracking data, visit history, or receipt images are ever transmitted over this connection.** All tracking logic remains strictly local.

## 5. Advertisements and Tracking
* **Zero Ads:** StoPoint is completely ad-free. We do not host or display third-party advertisements.
* **Zero Tracking:** We do not monitor your behavior, record your app usage patterns, or use third-party analytics services to track your activity.
* **No Analytics / No Telemetry:** We do not use Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics, Sentry, or any other telemetry tools. We have no way of knowing how you use the app or where you go. Your crashes and usage patterns are your own.

---

## 6. Data Retention and Deletion
* **User Control:** You can delete individual visits, documents, or places at any time within the app.
* **Full Wipe:** Uninstalling the app or "Clearing Data" in Android Settings will permanently delete all StoPoint data. **We cannot recover deleted data because we never had a copy.**
* **Portability:** You can export your data at any time via the JSON Backup, CSV, or PDF features.

StoPoint is a utility app intended for use by adults (**aged 18 and over**). We do not knowingly collect information from children.
