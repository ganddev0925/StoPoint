# Privacy Policy for StoPoint

**Effective Date:** February 25, 2026

### 1. Our Story and Promise
StoPoint was not built by a massive corporation; it was created by a solo developer. The app was originally designed as a personal tool to track our son's therapy classes—which happened several times a week—and calculate the time and costs associated with them. 

**Our Mission:** We know firsthand how expensive therapies and specialized care can be for families. Because of this, StoPoint is intentionally designed to be a "Zero Cost" app. There are no subscriptions, no ads, and no data-selling. We believe that a tool meant to help you manage your life shouldn't be another financial burden.

Because this app was built for my own family, privacy is its foundational principle. **StoPoint does not collect, harvest, sell, or upload your personal data to any external servers.** 

### 2. Information We Collect and How We Use It
To make StoPoint work seamlessly, the app requires certain permissions on your device. All data accessed by these permissions remains on your device.

* **Location Data (Including Background Location):** StoPoint requires access to your location, including in the background, to enable the Geofencing auto-tracking feature. This allows the app to automatically log when you arrive at or leave a saved place (like a clinic or workplace) without you having to open the app. *This location data is never shared with us or any third parties.*
* **Camera and Media/Photos:** If you use the OCR (Optical Character Recognition) feature to scan receipts or log costs, the app will request access to your camera or photo gallery. Image processing happens entirely on your device. We do not upload, view, or store your photos on any external servers.
* **Alarms and Notifications:** The app uses these permissions to remind you of active visits or to notify you when a visit is auto-logged.

### 3. Data Storage and Security
* **100% Local & Encrypted:** We do not have our own cloud servers. Every place you save, visit you log, and receipt you scan is stored securely and encrypted in a local database directly on your phone.
* **No Cloud Sync:** Because we do not use cloud storage, nobody (including the developer) can access your data. 
* **Your Data, Your Control:** You have full control over your data. The app provides built-in tools for you to manually export (backup) and import your data if you change devices.

### 4. Third-Party Services
While StoPoint does not share your data, we utilize secure, industry-standard third-party SDKs to provide core free features:
* **Google Maps SDK & Google Location Services:** We use these services to display maps and trigger geofencing. Your interactions with the map are subject to the [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### 5. Advertisements and Tracking
* **Zero Ads:** StoPoint is completely ad-free.
* **Zero Tracking:** We do not include third-party trackers or analytics that monitor your behavior. The app is supported purely through optional "Buy me a coffee" in-app purchases to support development.

### 6. Data Retention and Deletion
Because your data is only stored locally, you dictate how long it is kept.
* **To delete specific data:** You can delete individual visits or places directly within the app.
* **To delete all data:** You can wipe all your data instantly by clearing the app's data in your Android settings or by uninstalling the app.

### 7. Age Restrictions & Children’s Privacy
StoPoint is a general audience utility app intended strictly for use by adults (aged 18 and over). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are under 18, please do not use this app.
